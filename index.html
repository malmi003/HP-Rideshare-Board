Hogwarts Trip Planner

Best do it at a bit of a run if you're nervous

In a perfect world we would all take public transportation to Hogwarts. But for those of us running chronically late or early, sometimes ride sharing offers the only practical option.

We offer a platform to match your schedule with transportation currently being provided by our members. Just show up at King's Cross at the right time and catch a lift. Or enter your Rideshare information below to help others reach the castle.

At this time we're only servicing out of King's Cross. Come back soon for our expanded routes.
