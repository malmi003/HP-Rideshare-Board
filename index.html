How it works

In a perfect world we would all take public transportation to Hogwarts. But for those of us running chronically late or early, sometimes ride sharing offers the only practical option.

We offer a platform to match your schedule with transportation currently being provided by our members. Just show up at the right time and catch a lift or enter your Rideshare information below to offer it to others.

At this time we're only servicing out of King's Station, but we hear you! Come back in 2019 for our expanded routes.
